When I was a kid there was a specific feeling I would always get when some sort of voting procedure had to be chosen in school. The feeling was triggered every time I had to hear arguments like *"we shouldn't discuss this decision before voting because that would be influencing others"* or *"abstaining is vote manipulation"* or *"approval voting is unfair because it is not 'one person one vote"*.

Hearing normies talk about prediction markets is bringing those feelings back in full force.
